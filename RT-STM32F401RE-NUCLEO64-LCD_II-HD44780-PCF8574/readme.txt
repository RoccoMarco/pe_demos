** PLAY Embedded demos - HD44780 with Backpack on STM32 using ChibiOS/RT   **
*****************************************************************************

*** About ***
This demo runs on STM32 Nucleo-64 F401RE. The MCU drives a LCD 16x2 based 
on HD44780 with a I2C Backpack. The used RTOS is ChibiOS/RT.

*** Related article ***
For more information read the article "How to drive a HD44780 with I2C 
backpack with a STM32".
http://www.playembedded.org/blog/hd44780-backpack-stm32/

*** ChibiOS related version ***
Tested under ChibiOS trunk r12636
