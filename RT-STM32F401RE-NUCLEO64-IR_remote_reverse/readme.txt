** PLAY Embedded demos - IR remote with 21 button reverse engineering      **

*** About ***
This demo runs on STM32 Nucleo-64 F401RE. The aim of this demo is to perform
reverse engineering on the IR remote in order to understand how it works.

*** Related article ***
For more information read the article "IR Remote and STM32 using ChibiOS".
http://www.playembedded.org/blog/ir-remote-and-stm32-using-chibios/

*** ChibiOS related version ***
Tested under ChibiOS 21.11.1
