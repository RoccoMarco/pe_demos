*** About ***
This demo runs on STM32 Nucleo-64 F401RE. The aim of this demo is to explain
how use the touchpad based on TTP229B.

*** Related article ***
For more information read the article "Capacitive touchpad and ST Nucleo".
http://www.playembedded.org/blog/en/2015/07/21/capacitive-touchpad-and-st-nucleo/
