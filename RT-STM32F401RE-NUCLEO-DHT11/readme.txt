** PLAY Embedded demos - DHT11 on STM32                                    **
*****************************************************************************

*** About ***
This demo runs on STM32 Nucleo-64 F401RE. The aim of this demo is to explain
how to use a DHT11 sensor.

*** Related article ***
For more information read the article "Using DHT11 with ChibiOS/RT".
http://www.playembedded.org/blog/en/2015/06/23/using-dht11-with-chibiosrt/
