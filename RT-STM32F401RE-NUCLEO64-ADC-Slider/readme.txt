** PLAY Embedded demos - ADC and Slider                                    **
*****************************************************************************

*** About ***
This demo runs on STM32 Nucleo-64 F401RE. The aim of this demo is to explain
how ADC and PWM works in ChibiOS.

*** Related article ***
For more information read the article "Reading a Slider".
http://www.playembedded.org/blog/en/2015/08/07/reading-a-slider/
