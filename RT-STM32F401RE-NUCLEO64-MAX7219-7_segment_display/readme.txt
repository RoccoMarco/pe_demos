** PLAY Embedded demos - 7-Segment Display and STM32                       **
*****************************************************************************

*** About ***
This demo runs on STM32 Nucleo-64 F401RE. The aim of this demo is to drive
a 7-Segment display with a STM32 using ChibiOS.

*** Related article ***
For more information read the article "7-segment display and STM32 using
ChibiOS".
http://www.playembedded.org/blog/7-segment-display-and-stm32-using-chibios/

*** ChibiOS related version ***
Tested under ChibiOS 18.2.x Stable
