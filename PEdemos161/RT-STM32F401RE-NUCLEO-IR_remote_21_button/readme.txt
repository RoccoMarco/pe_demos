** PLAY Embedded demos - IR remote with 21 button                          **
*****************************************************************************

*** About ***
This demo runs on STM32 Nucleo-64 F401RE. The aim of this demo is to receive
data from a IR remote printing it on the UART 2.

*** Related article ***
For more information read the article "IR Remote and STM32 using ChibiOS".
http://www.playembedded.org/blog/en/2015/08/08/ir-remote-and-stm32-using-chibios/
