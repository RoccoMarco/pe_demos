*** About ***
This demo runs on STM32 Nucleo-64 F401RE. In this demo the STM32 gets data 
from the HC-SR04 displaying it on a HD44780 LCD 16x2 with I2C backpack.

*** Related article ***
For more information read the article "Detecting obstacles using an 
ultrasonic sensor HC-SR04".
http://www.playembedded.org/blog/en/2016/11/26/detecting-obstacles-hc-sr04/
